# Quiet Realms

Quiet Realms is the generative philosophy behind Original Collection 01. A
theme background should feel like a world continuing beyond the frame while
remaining quiet enough for work. Layered ridgelines, orbital paths, cloud
fields, sword vectors, moons, and small spirit forms emerge from mathematical
relationships rather than copied illustrations. The composition reserves a
low-information control area and lets visual energy gather on the opposite
side.

Every world begins with a stable seed. Pseudo-random variation controls ridge
rhythm, star placement, mist density, and small imperfections, but it always
operates inside deliberately narrow constraints. The same seed must reproduce
the same pixels, dimensions, hashes, and preview. Beauty is therefore part of a
reviewable computational process rather than a lucky one-off render.

The cinematic and chibi variants share a world-level color memory without
sharing a character design. Cinematic scenes favor scale, distance, and one
restrained visual vector. Chibi scenes translate the same atmosphere into
simple original geometry and a softer silhouette. This pairing is carefully
tuned to feel related while remaining free of identifiable costumes, faces,
logos, or franchise iconography.

Light and dark modes are treated as distinct system states, not filters. Each
state has its own sky, mist, ridge, glow, and interface tokens. The algorithm
maintains hierarchy across both states, and the validator checks that functional
colors retain readable contrast. Motion remains reduced by default; stillness
is part of the movement, not an accessibility afterthought.

The first release is intentionally a refined procedural starter collection.
Its generator favors reproducibility, calm composition, and transparent
craftsmanship over illustration-model spectacle. Future collections can add
more sophisticated fields, particles, recursive brush systems, or licensed
artwork, but each new technique must preserve the same master-level discipline:
purposeful parameters, original visual language, explicit provenance, and an
output that can be regenerated and audited.
