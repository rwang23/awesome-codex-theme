## Positioning decision

Background images and CSS alone are easy to copy. Awesome Codex Theme therefore
separates code-free theme packs from one fixed, reviewed Full Skin runtime. The
durable product is the standard, Registry, Validator, provenance, version-bound
real-app captures, Native fallback, and CI evidence. Gallery quality is the
front door, not the trust model.

The catalog mixes original worlds and cities with clearly disclosed,
non-commercial fan-art collections. Fan art never claims rights verification
and cannot use official screenshots, logos, promotional assets, or copied
compositions. A licensed catalog would need a separate verified rights class.
